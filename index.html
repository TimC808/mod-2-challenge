About Me

After over 30 years of serving in military and civilian leadership positions, I'm answering a calling I felt I've always missed in coding and web development. While I am highly adept at leading teams and projects, organizing for success, developing executable strategies, mitigating risk, and delivering results and profitability, I grew up in a period of significant transition that saw the birth of personal computers in the home and this thing called the internet. After I graduated from undergrad college, I would occassionally find a computer at work, but most work was still recorded with a pen or typewriter and a piece of paper. That aside, I am a proven leader of leaders skilled in talent management, human resources, logistics, sales and marketing and a growing set of Full Stack Web Development skills using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL, Github and other technologies. My goal is to venture in to the world of technology with a high degree of technical credibility while bringing significant strategic insights and experience to the table.
